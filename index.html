Hi, I'm David

I am a mechanical engineer with a passion for solving problems and designing consumer products. I love using mechatronics to make the everyday lives of people easier. Check out my portfolio for a summary of my past work, or projects/work for a more detailed perspective.

My background

Born in West Windsor New Jersey, I developed a passion for mechanical engineering from my hobby of modifying Nerf guns. Then, I moved onto a bachelors in mechanical engineering with a minor in electrical engineering at the University of Michigan. There, I worked on a student race car team and started making my first inventions. Now, I am pursuing my masters in mechanical engineering at Stanford where I am learning how to design better products.
